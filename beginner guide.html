Rule No. 1: Capital Preservation

BEGINNER'S MUST READ

4 Golden Pillars to Survive & Conquer the Markets

1

Strict 1% to 2% Risk Rule

If your total account balance is $1,000, your maximum loss on any single trade should never exceed $10 (1%). Beginners often blow their entire capital in a few trades due to oversized lot choices. Always consult the Lot Size Calculator before executing any trade!

2

The Trend is Your Friend

Never trade against the prevailing market momentum. If the market structure is in a clear Up-trend (Bullish), focus solely on finding Buy setups. If it is in a Down-trend (Bearish), look for Sell execution zones. Counter-trend trading is highly risky and often leads to unnecessary stop-out liquidations.

3

Eliminate Revenge Trading

Losses are an inevitable business expense in trading. If your Stop-Loss gets hit, accept the market's outcome and step away from the charts. Do not immediately enter random, emotional positions trying to "win back" your lost capital. Treat trading as an objective data-driven business, not an emotional gamble.

4

Avoid High-Impact News Events

Major institutional macro releases (such as FOMC, CPI, or NFP) create extreme volatility spikes that can completely override short-term technical setups. As a beginner, secure your capital by closing active positions before red-folder data releases. Monitor data cycles using our automated Live News Hub.
